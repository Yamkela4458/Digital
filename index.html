About Me

Yamkela Lusithi is a Digital Learning Developer with a background in early literacy education, learning design and research in language and learning. I am currently completing a B. Ed Honours in Language, Literacies and Literature (part time) at the University of Johannesburg and hold a Bachelor's degree in Foundation Phase Teaching. My work focuses on creating clear, accessible and visually structured learning content for young learners using digital design tools such as Figma, Canva and other learning development platforms. I apply research informed literacy principles, visual learning methods and structured content development practices to support comprehension, engagement and inclusive learning. I am interested in the intersection of education, design and technology and in developing learning experiences that improve understanding and access for diverse learners.

Educational Philosophy

I believe that meaningful learning occurs when content is clear, accessible and visually structured to support how learners process information. My approach draws on research informed literacy practices and evidence based learning design to create materials that are engaging, inclusive and developmentally appropriate. I focus on designing learning experiences that are intuitive, supportive and learner centred, ensuring that young learners can build understanding with confidence. Through continuous testing, reflection and refinement, I strive to develop digital and print learning resources that are effective, relevant and aligned with real learner needs.
